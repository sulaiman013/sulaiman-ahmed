Key Insight: Pipeline orchestration is nearly free at 0.0056 CU-hr per activity (~20 CU-seconds). Use Pipelines to orchestrate Notebooks and dbt Jobs, not for heavy data movement. Dataflow Gen2 Standard at 16 CU/sec is the most expensive per-second rate. Background operations get 24-hour smoothing, which is far more forgiving than Interactive's 5-64 minute window.
