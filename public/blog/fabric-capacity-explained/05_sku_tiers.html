  • 1 nightly dbt job + 1 small Notebook
  • A few pipelines moving data daily
~$524/mo ($17/day). Great for PoC or a team of 3 to 5 people testing Fabric.
⚡ F64: Mid-Size Production (Most Popular)
  • 100+ Power BI users across departments
  • 10+ dbt models running 2x daily + 5 Spark Notebooks
  • Dataflow Gen2 ingestion. Same as old Premium P1.
~$8,384/mo. Daily budget: 64 CUs × 1,440 min = 92,160 CU-min/day. Plenty for mixed workloads.
